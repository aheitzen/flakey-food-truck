Our pledge

Flake specializes in stuffed croissants that are both accessible and affordable.Flak'e’s unique sweet and savory croissants appeal to a broad range of tastes and flavor pallets. We have been serving the seattle community since 2003 and we plan on continueing this tradition of tasty treats for years to come. We love helping our community and seeing happy smiling faces as they eat our tasty treats,and we are sure you will find something you love as well!
