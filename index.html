Mission Statement

In 2008, Brandy quit her day job in LA to pursue her passion as a pastry perfectionist-- bringing her unique filled croissants to seattle where she grew up. Flake specializes in stuffed croissants that are both accessible and affordable.Flak'e’s unique sweet and savory croissants appeal to a broad range of tastes and flavor pallets, we have been serving the seattle community since 2003 and we plan on continueing this tradituion of tasty treats for years to come, here at flak'e we love helping our community and seeing the happy smiling faces as they eat our tasty treats,and we are sure you will find something you love as well!
